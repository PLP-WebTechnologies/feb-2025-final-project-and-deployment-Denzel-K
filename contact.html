Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to the blog?

We welcome guest contributions from writers who share our values and vision. Please send a brief pitch of your article idea to submissions@mindfulmusings.com along with some samples of your previous work.

Do you offer advertising or sponsorship opportunities?

Yes, we offer select advertising and sponsorship opportunities that align with our values. Please contact partnerships@mindfulmusings.com for more information about our rates and audience demographics.

How often do you publish new content?

We publish new articles three times a week, typically on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Subscribe to our newsletter to get notified when new content is available.

Can I republish your articles on my website?

Our content is protected by copyright. However, we do consider republication requests on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us with details about where and how you'd like to republish our work.
